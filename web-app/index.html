How to play

Select one of the highlighted legal cells in the grid

Your stone is added to that stack, then you sow the stack one stone at a time across the board

Choose highlighted orthogonal cells only, you can't move diagonally or immediately backwards to your last position

Four visible top stones in a row, column, or diagonal wins!
